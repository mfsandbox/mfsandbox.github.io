Intelligent Incentive


The Intelligent Incentive is targeted to an individual shopper and is a rich incentive encouraging them to purchase today and increase their spending.

Targeting can be based on tenure, recency of their last purchase, average spend, or any other available data to refine the incentive and call to action.

For example, if a shopper's average spend is $50, the call to action could be to earn a loyalty spend bonus when they spend $75 today.

Alternatively, if the shopper is lapsed or at-risk, we can deliver a 20% discount coupon, valid for today only.

The ability to test offers and incrementality of incentives is core to the Kinetic Thread platform
